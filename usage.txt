Scripts Overview

Profitability Ratios

Calculates Net Profit Margin, Operating Profit Margin, ROE, ROA, ROCE, and EPS
Creates trend graphs for the first five metrics and single number display for EPS


Liquidity Ratios

Calculates Current Ratio, Quick Ratio, and Cash Ratio
Creates trend graphs for Current and Quick Ratios, single number for Cash Ratio


Solvency Ratios

Calculates Debt-to-Equity Ratio, Interest Coverage Ratio, and Debt-to-Asset Ratio
Creates trend graphs for D/E and Interest Coverage, single number for Debt-to-Asset


Efficiency Ratios

Calculates Asset Turnover, Inventory Turnover, Receivables Turnover, and Days Sales Outstanding
Creates trend graphs for the turnover ratios and single number for DSO


Valuation Ratios

Calculates P/E Ratio, P/B Ratio, and EV/EBITDA
Creates trend graphs for P/E and P/B, single number for EV/EBITDA


Market Performance Ratios

Calculates Dividend Yield, Beta, and Market Capitalization
Creates trend graphs for Dividend Yield and Beta, single number for Market Cap


Combined Dashboard

Integrates all the individual ratio scripts into one comprehensive system
Creates a consolidated HTML report with all ratios, trends, and benchmarks
